The love of cooking was instilled in me at an early age by my parents and grandparents. They trensferred their passion for the culinary arts to me as the next generation to carry on their footsteps.

My first teachers were Papa Petar and Mama Blaga Oranski. They've proven to be as much of an inspiration today as when I was a little girl. I cannot thank them enough for their constant love, support, and the spirit of true hospitality which they shared with everyone. Without them, this book would not be possible. The wealth of memories they provided are responsible for this book. From Macedonia WithLoveTM is dedicated in honor of their memory.

Every Sunday, Mama and Papa orchestrated a magnificent feast from appetizers to desserts. My brother Vladimir, sister Sonja, and I began preperation for the meal several days in advance. We were encouraged to collect and "test out" new recipes in our family kitchen.

The purpose of From Macedonia with LoveTM is to serve as a culinary ambassador for the marvelous cuisine of my native Macedonia for our people and friends scattered throughout the globe.

May this volume inspire many delicious meals served with love and joy for many generations to come.

Dobar Apetit i Nazdravje.
To your good appitite in good health,


Marija Maya

Home Cooking With LoveTM is a vision we share in exploring new culinary horizons with our growing extended family and friends.

We begin each meal in prayer. We thank the Lord for His provisions as we enjoy the company of friends and family. It is no wonder that the Word of God, prayer, worship, and fellowship are identified as the means of Grace. It is the gathering together in community with thanksgiving which motivates and inspires Home Cooking With LoveTM.

Our purpose is to celebrate life through the joy of multicultural epicurean experience. International cooking and dining enables us to bring the entire world into our homes. It draws us together, bringing the gap between people from different lands and promoting better understanding as we share in the cooking and dining experience.

Our mission is to increase our collective appetite and appreciation for everything the Lord has made which is good. The Home Cooking with Love series begins with a joyful journey as we explore the cuisine of my native Macedonia From Macedonia with LoveTM.
